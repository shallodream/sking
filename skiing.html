Matters needing attention

Skiing equipment to wear complete, such as: gloves, hat, elbow protection, knee pads, hip protection, etc.; Especially for beginners should pay more attention to the equipment of protective gear, skincare products also need to be prepared, because the reflection of ultraviolet light is very severe snow.

For beginners to learn to ski must choose a suitable set of skiing equipment. The choice of snowshoes must be with the feet, the length of the ski and the length of the poles should be selected according to the height and weight of the individual.

Skiing is a physical exertion is more severe an aerobic exercise, in learning to ski to "do what you can" feel tired to rest in time, especially when children learn to ski parents or coaches must pay attention to the physical exertion of children.
